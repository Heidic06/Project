About

I started snowboarding about 4 years ago and it's become one of my favorite things to do. I'm no pro, I mostly just go to have fun and hang out with friends. There's nothing better than a good powder day with good company!

Tips for Beginners

  1. Get the right gear
    • Board, bindings, and boots
    • Helmet and wrist guards
    • Warm, waterproof clothes
  2. Learn the basics
    • Figure out if you're regular or goofy stance
    • Learn how to stop, very important!
    • Don't be afraid to fall, everyone does
  3. Just have fun
    • Go with friends
    • Take breaks when you need to
    • Hot chocolate at the lodge is always a good idea
